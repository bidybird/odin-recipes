Meatballs

This recipe is for full bodied meatballs that is not messing around with flavour. Some people may not believe a great meatball dish will utilize red sauce, but this one is the best!

The Sauce

This is the core of the recipe, good sauce requires time. Like eight hours of time.

The Meatballs

Your gonna get your hands dirty with these. Use gloves if your faint of heart while shaping them.

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • parmesan cheese
  • ground beef
  • ground pork
  • ground other meat if you have some
  • bread crumbs
  • a computer
  • some basil
  • salt and pepper for flavour
  • 3 cans of crushed tomatoes
  • chopped vegetables your choice
  • one pot and one pan

Steps

  1. Fill a pots base with some oil.
  2. Throw the chopped vegetables in there and let them soften up on low heat.
  3. Get a big bowl and put in your bread crumbs, your eggs, some parmesan, and your ground meat into it. Mix them together.
  4. Shape your meatballs in into spheres with an inch and a half diameter.
  5. In the pan, brown your meatballs. Get all sides of the meat. Do not neglect the veggies mix them around a bit.
  6. After the veggies soften up. Add the crushed tomatoes to the pot.
  7. Add the meatballs in the sauce and cook for almost forever.
