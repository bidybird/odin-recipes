Red Sauce Pasta

This recipe is for a full bodied pasta that is not messing around with flavour. Some people may not believe a great pasta dish will utilize red sauce, but this one is the bomb!

The Sauce

This is the core of the recipe. it has meat and olives. If you don't like olives take them out.

The Noodles

Get the noodles you like. Do not cook them too long though, they will have to hold up meat chunks.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pork ribs
  • olive oil
  • whatever vegetables you want to chop up
  • 20 olives
  • half an onion
  • some basil
  • salt and pepper for flavour
  • 3 cans of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 pots and one pan

Steps

  1. Fill a pots base with some oil.
  2. Throw the chopped vegetables in there and let them soften up on low heat.
  3. In the pan, brown your pork ribs. Get all sides of the meat. Do not neglect the veggies mix them around a bit.
  4. After the veggies soften up. Add the crushed tomatoes to the pot.
  5. You can figure the rest of the recipe out.
